Benefactors

Engineers

The Automatic Parking Lot Detection and Allocation System (APDAS) prototype is a significant opportunity for Electrical, Electronic, and Software Engineers to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. It demonstrates how core engineering principles can be applied to design and implement real-world automated systems, encouraging innovation in smart parking solutions and energy-efficient technologies. APDAS provides a platform for refining skills in sensor integration, actuator control, and software algorithm design, allowing engineers to solve real-world problems like traffic congestion and parking availability.

Car Drivers

The APDAS prototype offers a transformative parking experience by automating parking allocation and guidance, reducing driver frustration and stress. It streamlines the process with designated parking spots and real-time information, contributing to improved traffic flow in urban areas and enhancing overall mobility. The time saved by using the APDAS system can be used for more productive tasks.

Establishments with
Parking Facilities

The APDAS prototype offers easier parking and promises effective parking security, flexibility, and safety to improve customer satisfaction and increase productivity. The establishment's parking units will also attract more audience because of the APDAS and its innovative and eye-catching design and functions

Significance

Navigate Available Space Easier

Provides real-time guidance, allowing drivers to quickly locate and access the nearest available parking spot without unnecessary searching.

Minimize Time and Fuel Consumption

Helps decrease fuel wastage and emissions, promoting a more eco-friendly and cost-efficient parking experience.

Better Overall Parking Experience

Ensures a stress-free experience for drivers by eliminating common parking frustrations, such as long queues, misallocated spaces, and unnecessary delays.
